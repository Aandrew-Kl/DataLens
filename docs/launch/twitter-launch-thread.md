## Tweet 2 — the differentiator

The core trick: DuckDB-WASM runs analytical SQL in your browser over millions of rows. Zero backend round-trip for queries.

AI (NL → SQL) goes through your local Ollama. No OpenAI, no cloud. If you don't have Ollama, there's a rule-based fallback.
